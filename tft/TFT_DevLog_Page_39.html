23:14:26STATUS
Well, to sum it all up for now....
Everything is set to start on the Pyrus Pass dungeon.
The dungeon will not be very long, but this is the dungeon in which the main antagonist will be fought for the first time.
At the end of the game he'll be fought again in the final confrontation.
This will (of course) be a battle with significance.

In this time I'll also work out all spriting work for Krandar, which actuall has more priority than the dungeon and the boss itself.

Since Krandar will already leave the party permanently after this dungeon has been completed, I need to do this first, so that I will not forget it.

I am also planning to sprite Dandor's Spirata form as that will mean all spirata forms are drawn.

The animations come later though....

After this dungeon the final two masters will also be added to the game, and that will also be the moment the player will be able to get the item allowing him/her to open the Blue Seals and fight the boss inside, and the optional dungeon Star Dome, will be unlocked. Completing Star Dome will in turn unlock the Spirata Crypts.

In order words, there is still a lot to do before I can even THINK about the final boss fight

But I think it's clear that things are now definitely getting close to the final stage as the normal game content is concerned.

There are still lots of bugs and missing spell animations to take care of, and of course the New Game+, so this project is still far from finished, but we can say that an important milestone is in sight nevertheless!
