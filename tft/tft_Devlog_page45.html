15:04:19INTRODUCTION

Dreshka

Dreshka is a male Fairy and leader of the village-in-building with the same name, which is named after him (well, wasnt that obvious).

When you played the demo you know that four races: Humans, Elves, Fairies and Dwarves are at war which each other. Dreshka hated the war and basically deserted, and a lot of other Fairies with him. Under his lead a new Fairy Village is now being built near the Elfish Village of Iskarderiu, much to the fears of the Elves, but Dreshka did choose this spot on purpose. He wanted to be as far away from human and Fairy territory as possible as the war focusses most between the Humans and the Fairies. He hopes being close to the Elves he can avoid both races, and maybe even build a future bond with the Elves, as he knows he will have to trade with other communities anyway, someday.

Dreshka is a friendly man with a simple philosophy. War is bad. Whoever wants to forsake the war and live far away from it in peace is welcome. When the heroes of the game first meet him, he is still in the middle of building his village. Will he succeed in his goal to create a new Fairy Tribe living in peace?
