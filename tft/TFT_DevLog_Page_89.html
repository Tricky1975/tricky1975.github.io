01:11:29NOTE
One more final note, though.

I think I said this before, but I'll say it again. If you didn't see the new entries popping up on the times imprinted next to them, it does not mean they are false, they are in fact the times I created that entry in this log.

Unlike Star Story where a php script took note of my entries immediately and showed them immediately, the log in The Fairy Tale is written by means of an off-line DevLog utility which pushes all new entries once per 10 or 20 entries (it varies a bit) unless I FORCE it to push things through which I'll only do at the end of a programming session or when I really got something important to say. Also after uploading the site server may also have a small delay before everything is shown.

I do know that this takes the "live" part away from this log, but the server failures on sourceforge I had during the production of Star Story were so annoying and most of all threatening to this log I decided to this method. If this server crashes, well the log will remain up-to-date, it will only take a few days longer until you see it, well, who cares.

And yeah, I really hope that nobody was thinking that since this log is composed out of html files I was manually changing them all the time.... :-P
