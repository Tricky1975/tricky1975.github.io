20:15:16PLAN
I do plan on making a kind of "SDK" for LAURA II
You shouldn't expect too much from it, as there is nothing an SDK can offer that software already in existence cannot provide. LAURA II just uses PNG for its picture files, OGG for its Audio files, and Lua for scripting. Well there are already TONS of tools that can help you edit THAT kind of stuff.

Kthura and JCR6 are separate utilities already freely available on GitHub.

The only problem is that if you never used LAURA II for your projects before it can be a bit of a chore to set it all up. All this SDK will as such do is helping you bringing it all together and keep the hard work out of your way. It will also bring a simple "exporter" for Windows and Mac and you're on the road.
Using this "SDK" will take away some power I have myself, that's for sure, but it will only make things easier... (I think).
