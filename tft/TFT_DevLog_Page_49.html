Now some people claim well coded code contains NO "if"-statements at all. Why people claim something that stupid I don't know but at least they can be happy about the way the Grombo town has been scripted
function NPC_INNKEEPER()
  Inn()
end

function NPC_Sandra()
  MapText("SANDRA_" ..
            ({
               [false] = "A",
               [true] = "B"
            })[Done('&DONE.GROMBO.SPOKEN.SANDRA')]
         )
end

function QuendorGive()
   Var.D("$WMCHAT","QUENDOR")
   ItemGive("ZZKEY_GAIA")
end   

function NPC_Quendor()
       local ldone = Done("DONE.GROMBO.SPOKEN.QUENDOR")
       MapText("QUENDOR_"..upper(sval(ldone)));
       ({ [true] = Nothing, [false]=QuendorGive })[ldone]()       
end

function GALE_OnLoad()
    ZA_Enter('ToQuendor',function() GoToLayer('quendor',"Start") end)
    ZA_Enter("ToTown",function() GoToLayer('town',"Einde") end)
end

16:57:11INTRODUCTION

Feenalaria

And Feenalaria is one I must be careful about what I tell about her, as nearly all I can tell about her, could be spoilers. Well-seen, shes a Fairy and yes she does indeed look a lot like Marrilona, and yes that was intentional.
When you have completed the Secrets of Dyrt youve already met her, as she succeeded the Fairy Elder of the Fairy Tale in his position, and due to that she was the Fairy Elder in the events during Dyrt (please note this game takes place 200 years prior to Dyrt, and Fairies can get quite old).
Those who played Star Story and managed to unlock the world of Phantasar will have met her in that game in the role as Fairy Elder as well.

I guess we could say that Feenalaria has become a bit of a recurring character in my RPG games.

I can give away a few characteristics. Feenalaria, nicknamed Feena, is a very strong-willed and intelligent woman, with a few tomboyish features. She has the spirit of a warrior and protects those she loves like a lioness. She also has a strong sense of justice and is also pretty well able to handle herself in politics, and she does not easily give up. If she has a fault, it could be that she cant accept that there are always people who hate others for whatever reason, and that it is impossible to make everybody happy.
