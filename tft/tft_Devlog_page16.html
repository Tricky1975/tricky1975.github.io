20:38:58TECHNOThe blockmap is easier to explain. It decides where you can go and where you cannot.

The pathfinder also uses this to plan its routes when you control your character by mouse.

It's easy to guess that when poorly used you may get 'invisble barriers' or stuff you can go through while it should be blocked.

20:37:32TECHNODominance is a very important feature in Kthura. Incorrect usage of dominance will result into very terrible levels. It decides what goes more up front and what not. In the Cathedral the dominance was used to make the doorways act like real doorways and thus the upper side will always be in front of the player sprite giving the illusion you go under it.

The same trick was used on the walls in the spaceport in Nizozemska in Star Story.
