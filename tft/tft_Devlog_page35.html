21:21:50ADWell time fore a little advertisement

This is the comic of a loser of a guy (Arthur) living with a Hipster Girl (Sophie) and a Gamer Girl (Erika) in one house.

Arthur is just a normal guy. A bit of a loser, and a bit of trouble keeping his sexual interest for the girls he lives with under control. Erika in particular gives him a hard time.

Erika is a gamer girl who is only interested in sleeping and games. She looks good and is ignorant about it and totally doesn't realise she often embarrases Arthur greatly (and it's hard to believe she has no romantic feelings for him)

Sophie is the hipster girl. Very intelligent, but also very cynical. The worst part is that in 99% of the cases she's right about her cynical views, but a little more tact would be needed. She's also a true fan of Apple and she always wants Apple's newest model phones and tablets.

The comic as a whole makes a joke in a rather cynical way with modern times. I love these jokes. You will have to forgive the bad English sometimes. These comics are originally written in Spanish. To be frank I'm glad the author took the trouble to translate them at all, so I can understand the dark humor.
