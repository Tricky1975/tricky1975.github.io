11:34:33MISC
Like said before, today will be fully dedicated to Marrilona.

Well, Marrilona will require a special workout.

Not only is Marrilona the only female character among the main team (well her gender will not require any extra work), she's also the only character fully devoted to magic.

In the original game, this didn't really matter and her wands served as physical weapons. Well if you think about it, that does not really make sense, does it? A wand is a magic weapon after all....

As a result I have decided for this game, that Marrilona should therefore have their own unique set of wands all containing a special spell she can cast for free as long as the wand is equipped. The best part is also that she will be able to use these wands even when she is silenced.

Due to this there will also be only two cities in the game were you can buy weapons for Marrilona. Frendor and Nostraburg respectively, which I deem quite logical as they are the two main Fairy villages you'll visit on your journey.

Now all (main) characters will be able to get their ultimate weapons from a sealed optional boss, Marrilona is no exception, but you can imagine a pretty powerful spell will be attached to the wand she will obtain from the sealed bosses.

To make this all possible, various changes are required to make this possible, so it may take some time before Marrilona herself actually makes her appearance in the game. The first things you see happening may have nothing to do with her, but trust me, they have ;)
