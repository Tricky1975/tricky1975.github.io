Tomorrow I'll make the start to the first flashback part. Two dedicated characters will need to be created for this part, but I cannot reveal anything about them due to SPOILERS!!!

Creating new characters has always been nasty business though, so I'll need to be careful.

The statistics of your main party will form the basis of both characters, and the moves they learned can be used to determine the moves you can use during the flashbacks.
Although it does not seem realistic the main party and the flashback party will share their inventories.
I don't want to be any harder than need be.

The first flashback is also the only one requiring a map where Jake and the others won't come during the main quest, however, for completionist's sake this flashback will unlock this map on the worldmap, so after the flashback duo completes it Jake and the others will be able to visit it to claim missed treasure.
The plan is to make the Traveler's emblem there only available to you when you actually visit this place with Jake and the others.
The same should happen to the other two flashbacks (that contain a dungeon, as there is also a "boss only" flashback).
