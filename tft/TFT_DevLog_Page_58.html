13:59:03INTRODUCTION

Krandar, son of Gandophar

Krandar, son of Gandophar is one of the last important key characters you will meet during the story, and maybe the most important character of the story as a whole, as well.

Krandar is the leader of a tribe called "The Black-Winged Fairies", a warrior tribe with strong magic skills. Everybody, the Fairies in particular, fear them, as Krandar and his men have a reputation as notorious homicidal terrorists. The truth is that nobody actually knows what kind of guy he is, and what his motives are and what kind of activities his tribe is in. All people know is that Krandar was chosen by his people to lead them (which is pretty remarkable as democracy is not very well-known to the people on Phantasar, at least not to the tribes discusses in this story), and that he and his men live in the village of Nostraburg on the continent of Kokonora, and that his men are extremely loyal to him.

Wether he is good or evil is something for you to find out. If hes good, hell be an asset to your heroes on your quest. If bad, hell be a terrible enemy.

Do you have the guts to try him out?
