Time for the scratch list:
Well things will go like this
  1. First of all building the release, which is currently in process
  2. Then the Mac version will be uploaded
  3. Then the Windows version will be uploaded
  4. Once that's done the announcement will be officially made.
  5. While people are testing the game, I will in the meantime develop Dandleton
  6. Flashback 1
  7. Flashback 2
  8. Flashback 3
  9. Flashback 4
  10. Flashback 5
  11. And once Dandleton is done I'll first tike about a week off from this project, as I really need a short vacation from it
  12. Then I'll begin fixing all issues milestoned as "Open Beta", stuff people report in the meantime will be judged for severity and then be milestoned for the open beta or the stable launch
  13. Once all Open beta issues are fixed, the game will get out of the Early access mode, and the game will then be fully released, but still be marked as beta
  14. Depending on my needs another break may be in order
  15. And then the final issues will be fixed
  16. Once my issue tracker is empty (not counting issues on it that are not specific for this game), the game will be deemed "stable" and the guy holding the checkered flag may appear in this DevLog.

  18. The IndieDB and itch releases will only be done when the stable release is out. On sourceforge and homebrew the Open Beta may be released already, stuff I'm currently still researching will determine how often, though.
